The one I'm proudest of. I designed, built, and shipped a full-stack Solana marketplace end to end — product, engineering, and operations, all of it mine.

JavaScript and Node.js on Firebase. Secure auth with Firebase Authentication, a Firestore backend served through serverless Cloud Functions and REST APIs, and on-chain SOL transactions powering every payment. It grew to 350+ active users and over $200,000 in revenue. Seven months after launch, I negotiated its sale.

I still own the domain and all rights. The sale was the first chapter — a relaunch is already in the works, bigger and better.
